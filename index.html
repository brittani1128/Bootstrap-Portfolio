About Me

Hi! Welcome to my page. My name is Brittani (Britt) and I was born and raised in Austin, Texas. I went to school at UT in Austin where I studied biology and played volleyball on the club team. I have a Masters in marine biology from Texas A&M in Galveston. After school I studied environmental consulting, and then went into sales. While in my sales job I began dabbling with coding through on online program, and from there knew I wanted to pursue it full time!

In April this year I quit my job and backpacked solo around SE Asia to Thailand, Cambodia, and Vietnam. It was the most incredible experience of my life to date, but I know there will be many more like it to come! I love traveling and can't wait to have a career that allows me to work more independently, and that's also interesting and challenging. That being said, as an athlete for most of my life I enjoy working in collaborative, team environments as well. I am most interested in pursuing front end development as I enjoy being creative and learning about the latest technologies to enhance user experience.

In my free time you can find me out and about with my pup, playing sand or indoor volleyball, at the gym, or checking out new restaurants. I also have a passion for coaching volleyball to younger generations.
