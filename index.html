About Me

Hi! My name is Brittani (Britt) and I was born and raised in Austin, Texas. I went to school at UT in Austin where I studied biology and played volleyball on the club team. I have a Masters in marine biology from Texas A&M in Galveston. After school I studied environmental consulting, and then went into sales. While in my sales job I began dabbling with coding through on online program, and from there knew I wanted to pursue it full time!

In April this year I quit my job and backpacked solo around SE Asia to Thailand, Cambodia, and Vietnam. It was the most incredible experience of my life to date, but I know there will be many more like it to come! I love traveling and can't wait to have a career that allows me the finances and flexibility to travel regularly, but that's also interesting and challenging.

In my free time you can find me out and about with my pup, usually at Red Bud, Barton Springs, Zilker, or anywhere else with water. I also love to play volleyball, both sand and indoor, and regularly get in the gym to lift heavy sh*t.
